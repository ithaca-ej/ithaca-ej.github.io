2022 Toronto/Hamilton Photos

I was a first year at Niagara College back then — Provoke, Ed Van Der Elsken and Daido Moriyama my muse and the world my oyster. Contrary to the digital photography program I was in, I preferred to shoot and print on film. It was also my first time living on my own. Perhaps that is why I can no longer replicate these pictures.
