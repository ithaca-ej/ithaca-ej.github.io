My first ever college film assignment! I had recently transferred from the digital photography program, so I was a little nervous at first — but the film came out great regardless! Despite it's simplicity, the whole thing took four hours to shoot...

I was really into Soviet cinema at the time, so the whole thing ended up becoming an exercise in cutting-on-action. It's amazing how much expression can be created with just the montage!
