Jazz

Around that same time, I still clung to my high school dream of becoming the greatest harmonica player of all-time. Even after my best friend had given up on said dream, and even after my high school rival had long since became a label within the independant Hamilton rock scene. I didn't leave any notes behind, so I no longer remember their names, but their faces have been imprinted in these negatives for as long as time will allow them. I wonder how they're all doing now?
