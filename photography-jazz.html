2022 Jazz Photos

Around that same time, I still clung to my high school dream of becoming the greatest harmonica player of all time. I couldn't learn jazz quick enough, so I figured photography would suffice until my skills caught up. They never did.

I'd never bothered to take notes during shooting, so their names are lost to me now. A moment forever trapped in time. I wonder how they're all doing now?
